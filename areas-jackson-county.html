About the County

Working ranch country at the southern edge of the Texas Triangle.

Jackson County sits directly south of Wharton County, on the southern edge of the Texas Triangle. Edna is the county seat, on US-59 about 90 minutes south of Houston. The county is rural, agricultural, and quiet — the kind of working ranch country that draws cattle buyers, 1031 investors, and recreational landowners who want acreage without the traffic that comes with closer-in counties.

The land is mostly cattle, rice, and row-crop country with active hunting markets. Properties trade as working ag land, weekend ranches, hunting acreage, and small-town homes in Edna, Ganado, La Ward, Vanderbilt, and Lolita. The county is one of the more 1031-friendly markets in this part of Texas, with working ag-exempt parcels in steady supply.

Ag exemption is widely available under cattle, hay, row crops, rice, or wildlife management. J4LP confirms current ag status and what it takes to keep or transfer the exemption before closing.

Common Questions

What buyers and sellers ask about Jackson County.

Specifics that come up before contracts get signed.

Where is Jackson County, Texas?

South Texas, directly south of Wharton County, on the southern edge of the Texas Triangle. County seat: Edna, on US-59. About 90 minutes south of Houston, two hours from San Antonio.

What towns are in Jackson County?

Edna (county seat), Ganado, La Ward, Vanderbilt, and Lolita. Several smaller rural communities scattered across the county. Edna sits on US-59, the main corridor through the area.

What kind of land is for sale here?

Working cattle ranches, rice fields, row-crop farmland, hay pasture, hunting and recreational acreage, homestead-size parcels, and small-town homes. Active 1031 market.

Is ag exemption available?

Widely. Most rural acreage qualifies under cattle, hay, row crops, rice, or wildlife management. We confirm current ag status and what it takes to keep or transfer the exemption before closing.

How far is Jackson County from Houston?

Edna is about 90 minutes south of Houston via US-59. The county is one of the closer rural counties for Houston-area buyers looking for working ranch land or 1031-friendly acreage.

Do you list off-market properties in Jackson County?

Often. We work directly with landowners and hear about ranches and ag land before they list publicly. Tell us what you need by acreage range, town, or property type.
