Field Notes

Straight talk on the towns, land, water, schools, and counties we live in. Written by the local agents who know them, not a search portal.

What This Is

One Community Connector per town. Not one map for all of it.

Rural South Texas is not one place. El Campo is not Hallettsville. Bay City is not Bellville. The water table, the school districts, the ag exemption rules, the flood plain, the drive times, the people, the prices. All of it changes county to county and town to town.

So we are not writing one blog that pretends to cover all of it from a desk. We are building Field Notes the way we run the brokerage: a local agent who lives in a community writes about that community. El Campo is where we started, because that is home. It is the first community here, not the only one.

As we grow the J4 Heritage Group, every new market gets its own Community Connector, an agent who lives there, works there, and knows the back roads. When they join, their town gets its own field notes. Watch this page fill in.
