Our Story

It started with a fence crew
and a handshake.

In 1994, Cuatro Strack started building fences for Texas ranch owners. Not as a stepping stone to something else, as a calling. He learned land from the ground up, literally. How fences fail. Where water runs. What a property costs to maintain versus what it costs to improve. What ranch owners actually need versus what they're usually sold.

Over three decades, that single fencing operation grew into an ecosystem of eight companies, each one built to solve a real problem that J4 clients were running into. Water wells when buyers needed water. Manufactured homes when families needed affordable housing. Storage when people were moving. Power when properties went dark.

The thread connecting all of it: a family that shows up, does the work, and doesn't disappear when the job is done.

1994

J4 Fencing & Services Founded

Cuatro Strack starts building fences for Texas ranch owners. High-security, coastal construction, and government contracts follow.

2000s

Water Works & Real Estate Added

J4 Water Works launches to serve the drilling, septic, and water treatment needs of ranch buyers. J4 Real Estate begins investment property acquisition.

2010s

White Wing Storage, White Wing Estates, and High Standard Power Solutions added to serve the full spectrum of J4 client needs.

2019

J4 Cattle Established

J4 Cattle brings livestock operations into the ecosystem. El Campo, TX. Another piece of the full-circle land legacy mission.

Why It Matters

What the ecosystem means for your transaction.

No other brokerage in the Wharton County market can offer this. When you work with J4 Legacy Properties, you get the whole family.

01

You see the real cost of ownership

Before you close on a property, we can tell you, with real numbers from J4 Fencing and J4 Water Works, exactly what it will cost to fence it, drill a well, or improve the infrastructure. No surprises in year one.

02

You have trusted contractors from day one

After close, you don't start from zero trying to find a fencing company or a water well driller. The J4 team is already there. People who know your property, your water, and your land because they helped you evaluate it.

03

Sellers get a marketing edge

We can include J4 Fencing or J4 Water Works improvements in a listing package, giving buyers confidence about the infrastructure and giving sellers a stronger price justification. No other broker can do this.

04

1031 buyers get income analysis built in

We know what ag land leases for, what grazing rates look like, and what properties cost to operate, because J4 Cattle runs its own operations. Those are real numbers, not estimates pulled from a trade magazine.

05

Community families land with support

Moving to the country and need a storage unit during the transition? A well inspection? A generator installed before the first big storm? White Wing Storage, J4 Water Works, and High Standard Power are all one phone call away.

06

It's accountability, not just service

When the same family owns the fencing company, the water company, and the real estate brokerage, there's nowhere to hide. Our reputation across all eight businesses is built or broken by how we treat every single client.

The Family Behind It

This isn't a holding company.

Cuatro and Stephanie Strack built the J4 Companies for their clients, and for their kids. Every business in the ecosystem was started because a J4 client needed something and there wasn't a trustworthy option available. So Cuatro built one.
