About This Property

Nestled on the Pearl of the Prairie in Limestone County, this expansive 25-acre estate delivers luxurious and versatile living unlike anything else on the market in the region. The main house spans 5,000 square feet with five spacious bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms — space built for family, not just aesthetics.

A 1,000-square-foot detached garage apartment sits adjacent to the main house, ideal for a home office or guest accommodations. Out back, a crystal-clear swimming pool sits beneath a large travertine-tiled porch, the kind of outdoor space you use year-round in South Texas.

The property's barn complex houses a two-bedroom barndominium, a bunkhouse, and a state-of-the-art gym. Beyond the structures, you'll find covered parking, extensive storage, two additional barns for livestock and equipment, a charming three-bedroom two-bathroom farmhouse, and a tranquil stocked pond that anchors the back of the property.

This is the kind of property you hand down, not sell.
