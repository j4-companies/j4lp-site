Why This Seminar

You've been thinking about it for a while.

Trading the cul-de-sac for some acreage. A place the kids can run. Room for the dogs. Maybe a few chickens, maybe a horse, maybe more.

The trouble is, nobody's giving you straight answers. Where the water comes from. What septic costs. Why ag exemption matters more than you think. Whether the road to your front gate is yours or your neighbor's.

We've been in this market for 22 years across the J4 family of companies. We've fenced it, drilled it, built on it, sold it, and lived on it. This is a real conversation about what you'd actually be signing up for.

What You'll Learn

60 minutes. The questions most agents skip.

The honest question before you buy Do you actually want to live on land, or visit it on weekends? Two different things. The wrong answer costs about $250,000.
What 10 acres in Wharton or Matagorda actually costs Land price, water well, septic, electric to property line, fencing. The full picture before you start scrolling Zillow.
The ag exemption advantage (and what it requires) What it can do for your tax bill, what counties actually look for, and what gets it pulled.
The 4 family readiness questions that matter Driving, work, problem-solving, community. Before you fall in love with acreage, every family needs an honest look.
Live Q&A 20 minutes of real questions, real answers. Bring the ones you're actually carrying around.
Who This Is For

Built for families thinking about the move.

  • Families with kids who want more room to breathe and more land to grow on
  • Remote workers who've figured out the office isn't the point anymore
  • Suburban homeowners tired of HOA rules and small lots
  • Anyone who's Googled "acreage for sale in Texas" at 11 PM and needs the real picture

This is not for people looking to flip property fast or investors chasing short-term returns. This is for families who want to plant roots.
